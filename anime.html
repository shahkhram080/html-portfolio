Top 5 animes that I watched

  1. Demon Slayer

    2. Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba is a popular anime series that follows the story of Tanjiro Kamado, a young boy whose family is slaughtered by demons, leaving him and his younger sister Nezuko as the sole survivors. Nezuko is turned into a demon, which sets Tanjiro on a quest to become a Demon Slayer and find a way to turn her back into a human.

  2. Jujutsu kaisen

    3. Jujutsu Kaisen follows high school student Yuji Itadori, who joins a secret organization of Jujutsu Sorcerers to eliminate a powerful Curse named Ryomen Sukuna. The story explores themes of curses, sorcery, and the supernatural as Yuji navigates his new life and responsibilities.

  3. Chainsawman

    4. Following a betrayal, a young man left for dead is reborn as a powerful devil-human hybrid after merging with his pet devil and is soon enlisted into an organization dedicated to hunting devils. When his father died, Denji was stuck with a huge debt and no way to pay it back.

  4. Scissor seven

    5. SCISSOR SEVEN chronicles the adventures of a fourth-rate assassin who attempts to use disguises and a pair of hairdressing scissors to complete his missions. Wu Liuqi (Ronny Chieng) lost his memory two years ago, and he wants to recover it.

  5. One punch man

    6. One Punch Man is a Japanese superhero webcomic created by ONE. It follows Saitama, the main protagonist, who possesses immense strength and can defeat any opponent with a single punch. This overwhelming power leads him to an existential crisis, as he struggles with boredom and a lack of challenge in battles. The series blends action and comedy while exploring themes of heroism and the nature of strength in a world filled with superhuman beings.
